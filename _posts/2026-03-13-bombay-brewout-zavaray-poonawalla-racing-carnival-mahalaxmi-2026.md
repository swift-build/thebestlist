Some events exist to be attended. The Zavaray S. Poonawalla Racing Carnival, Bombay Brewout Edition, is one of them.

Returning on Sunday, 15th March at Mumbai's iconic Mahalaxmi Racecourse, this is the rare kind of day out that manages to be genuinely everything at once. A heritage racing venue. Elite thoroughbreds thundering down the track. Over 50 craft brews on tap. Gourmet food pop-ups. Live bands. A funky flea market. And the kind of crowd that turns up dressed well and stays until the very last pour.

**The Racing**

Hosted in association with the Royal Western India Turf Club, the carnival keeps Mumbai's storied racing legacy very much at its centre. This is not a music festival with horses in the background. The racing is real, the energy is electric, and the sight of elite thoroughbreds coming down the final stretch at Mahalaxmi is something that never gets old, whether it is your first time or your fifteenth.

**The Brewout**

Where the Bombay Brewout Edition earns its name is in the craft beer offering, which is serious. Over 50 craft brews on tap means there is no universe in which you do not find something worth lingering over. Pair that with gourmet food pop-ups and a flea market worth actually browsing, and the spaces between races become just as compelling as the races themselves.

**The Vibe**

Live bands set the tone across the afternoon, and the dress code, Western Formals, means the crowd arrives with intention. This is a day that rewards dressing up, showing up early, and staying late.

**Event Details**

Date: Sunday, 15th March 2026

Time: 12:00 PM onwards

Venue: Mahalaxmi Racecourse, Mumbai

Dress Code: Western Formals
