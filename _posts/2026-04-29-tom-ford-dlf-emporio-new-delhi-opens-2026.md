There is a particular quality to walking into a Tom Ford space. It announces itself before you have properly entered: the palette of deep grey and warm brown, the chrome catching the light at precisely the angle it was designed to, the sense that the air itself has been considered. It is an environment that does not ask you to slow down so much as it makes slowing down feel inevitable.

That environment has arrived in New Delhi.

Tom Ford Fashion opened the doors of its newest boutique at DLF Emporio on 26 February 2026, bringing the complete world of the house to the capital in a space that is, by any measure, the most significant luxury fashion opening the city has seen in recent memory. The boutique occupies the ground floor of Emporio with the assurance of a brand that has never needed to raise its voice to make itself heard.

**The Space**

The interior is modelled after the brand's Madison Avenue flagship in New York, which is to say it is impeccable. The signature Tom Ford palette of greys and deep brown plays against chrome and glass with the precision of a house that has spent two decades understanding exactly how its aesthetic should feel in three dimensions. Display cabinets in Cala Paonazzo marble and Macassar ebony hold the collections with the reverence that the materials deserve. Discreetly partitioned walls create the sense of privacy that genuine luxury has always required and that so few retail environments actually manage to deliver.

It is a space that does not perform exclusivity. It simply embodies it.



![](/assets/images/posts/dsc03287-edit.jpg)



**The Edit**

The New Delhi boutique presents the full range of Tom Ford Fashion, which under the creative direction of Haider Ackermann, appointed in 2024, is in the midst of one of the more quietly compelling evolutions in contemporary luxury. Ready-to-wear for both men and women sits alongside leather goods, fragrances, beauty, eyewear, and underwear: the complete Tom Ford world, nothing abbreviated.

Ackermann's arrival at the house has been the subject of considered attention from the fashion world. His instinct for precise, sensual tailoring and his sensitivity to fabric and drape align with the Tom Ford DNA in ways that feel organic rather than imposed. The collections arriving at Emporio carry that intelligence, and the New Delhi edit will give the capital its first extended encounter with the house in its current, distinctly compelling form.

**The Made-to-Measure**

The detail that distinguishes the DLF Emporio boutique from a simple retail opening is the inclusion of Tom Ford's made-to-measure service, available through private one-on-one appointments. The process is what the word bespoke was coined to describe before it became a synonym for premium pricing: master tailors at the Tom Ford atelier devoting weeks of craftsmanship to cutting, constructing, and finishing a men's suit in fabric from the world's finest mills, built to the specific measurements and requirements of the person who will wear it.

For New Delhi, a city with a serious and longstanding relationship with tailoring culture, the arrival of this service carries a particular resonance. The Tom Ford made-to-measure suit is not simply a garment. It is an argument about what the relationship between a man and his clothes can be when both parties are operating at the highest possible level of seriousness.



![](/assets/images/posts/dsc03320-edit.jpg)



**Why It Matters**

The opening of a Tom Ford boutique in any city is a statement about that city. It is a house that does not expand casually, and its choice of DLF Emporio as its New Delhi home reflects both the quality of that address and the brand's assessment of the Indian luxury market's direction of travel.

That assessment is well-founded. The Indian luxury consumer in 2026 is more sophisticated, more informed, and more demanding than at any previous point. They have travelled, they have shopped internationally, and they return with the expectation that what is available to them in Paris and New York should be available to them at home. Tom Ford at Emporio is, in the most direct sense, a response to that expectation.

The boutique is located at 126-132, Ground Floor, DLF Emporio, Nelson Mandela Marg, Vasant Kunj, New Delhi.
