Mannatt Gupta unveils ***Ethereal Realms***, a collection that cements the label’s position as one of India’s most compelling contemporary RTW voices—where imagination, craftsmanship and modern femininity converge.

Rooted in celestial lore yet grounded in the realities of today’s woman, *Ethereal Realms* explores fashion as fantasy and power in equal measure. Fluid drapes meet architectural cuts, creating silhouettes that move effortlessly while holding quiet strength. There’s a sense of theatricality throughout—an otherworldly elegance that feels intentional, never costume-like. Think of it as a fantastical expedition through time, charging boldly into fashion’s future.

Mythology and cosmic symbolism weave subtly through the collection: wing-inspired accents, crown-like structures, and celestial motifs appear with restraint and refinement. A palette of soft pastels and nuanced neutrals is elevated with glints of gold and silver, while layered fabrics and tactile contrasts lend depth—hallmarks of Mannatt Gupta’s signature aesthetic.



![](/assets/images/posts/attachment-1.jpeg)



**Mannatt Gupta, Designer & Founder says,**

> *Ethereal Realms is about bringing together strength and softness, tradition and today’s world. Every piece is made to move naturally, offering structure, comfort, and a quiet feeling of confidence. The celestial elements reflect the inner worlds women hold, reminders of our grace, resilience, and light.*

A consistent favourite among India’s most influential style icons, the brand has been worn by **Deepika Padukone, Malaika Arora**, and several leading Bollywood actresses, and has earned editorial recognition across Indian and international news agencies.

With *Ethereal Realms*, Mannatt Gupta continues to redefine Indian RTW—romantic yet powerful, fantastical yet wearable—making a compelling case for why the label remains a mainstay for celebrities, stylists, and editors alike.
